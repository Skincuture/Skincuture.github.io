Microneedling

Microneedling

What is Microneedling?

Microneedling is a minimally invasive treatment that uses fine needles to create micro-injuries, stimulating collagen and elastin production.

It improves skin texture, firmness, and overall appearance.

What does the treatment include?

  • Skin consultation and analysis
  • Cleansing and preparation
  • Microneedling procedure
  • Application of active serums
  • Soothing and hydrating care
  • SPF protection

Benefits

  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Improves texture and tone
  • Reduces fine lines
  • Minimizes pores and acne scars
  • Enhances skin firmness

Who is it for?

Suitable for uneven texture, acne scars, wrinkles, enlarged pores, and dull skin.

Results

Skin becomes smoother, firmer, and more radiant. Best results are achieved with a course of treatments.

PRP / PRF

Microneedling with PRP / PRF

What is PRP?

PRP (Platelet-Rich Plasma) uses your own blood plasma rich in growth factors to stimulate skin regeneration.

What is PRF?

PRF is an advanced version of PRP with a higher concentration of growth factors and longer-lasting effects.

What does the treatment include?

  • Consultation and analysis
  • Blood draw and PRP/PRF preparation
  • Skin preparation
  • Microneedling
  • PRP/PRF application
  • Soothing care and SPF

Benefits

  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Improves texture and tone
  • Reduces wrinkles and acne scars
  • Enhances skin elasticity
  • Natural rejuvenation

Results

Skin becomes smoother, firmer, and more radiant over time.

Carboxytherapy

Carboxytherapy

What is Carboxytherapy?

Carboxytherapy is a non-invasive treatment that uses carbon dioxide (CO₂) to stimulate circulation, improve oxygenation, and enhance skin regeneration.

It helps revitalize the skin, improve tone, and boost overall skin health.

What does the treatment include?

  • Skin consultation and analysis
  • Cleansing and preparation
  • Application of carboxy gel and mask
  • CO₂ stimulation process
  • Finishing care
  • SPF protection

Benefits

  • Improves blood circulation
  • Brightens and evens skin tone
  • Reduces dullness and dark circles
  • Stimulates collagen production
  • Enhances skin elasticity
  • Promotes natural glow

Who is it for?

Suitable for all skin types, especially dull, tired, uneven, or aging skin.

Results

Skin appears brighter, smoother, and more refreshed. Regular sessions improve long-term skin health.
